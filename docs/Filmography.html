Let's Go Away - 2024

A boy and a girl, each lost in their thoughts in separate rooms, tune in to the same enchanting song, sparking a shared desire to escape together. Their dream takes an unexpected turn when a peculiar Uber driver jolts them back to reality, revealing that their connection exists only in their daydreams. Despite the revelation, they decide to embrace the fleeting moments, walking side by side, navigating the bittersweet terrain of longing, and cherishing the remaining time in this liminal space.
